About ASPL

Altener Solutions Private Limited (ASPL) is a Bangalore-based commercial electric vehicle company focused exclusively on the 3-wheeler cargo segment. With over 5–6 years of dedicated in-house research and validation, ASPL has developed scalable EV architectures engineered for Indian commercial conditions. Our philosophy is engineering-first. Every subsystem — from chassis structure and drivetrain integration to battery management and electronics — is internally designed, tested and refined to deliver high durability, operational efficiency and lower total cost of ownership. ASPL aims to position itself among leading commercial EV manufacturers by combining technology depth, manufacturing scalability and fleet-readiness into a unified commercial mobility platform.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become a leading commercial EV platform company in India by delivering engineering-driven, high-performance cargo vehicles that compete with established industry leaders in durability, lifecycle reliability and operational efficiency.

Our Mission

To design, develop and scale intelligent electric cargo platforms that reduce operational costs, enhance fleet productivity and accelerate the transition toward sustainable commercial mobility across urban and semi-urban markets.

Research & Development

R&D is the core foundation of ASPL. Over the last 5–6 years, our multidisciplinary engineering team has focused on developing next-generation commercial EV systems using modern automotive technologies. Our development programs include advanced battery management systems (BMS), telematics-enabled fleet analytics, RFID-based vehicle identification, IoT integration, embedded control systems, and predictive maintenance architecture.
