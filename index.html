About ASPL

Altener Solutions Private Limited (ASPL) is a Bangalore-based EV startup focused on manufacturing intelligent electric three-wheelers for passenger and cargo mobility. Our mission is to build scalable, affordable and sustainable transportation solutions for the future of urban India.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To lead India’s transition toward clean electric mobility by delivering reliable and high-performance EV solutions.

Our Mission

To design, manufacture, and scale EV 3-wheeler platforms that redefine last-mile transportation and logistics.
