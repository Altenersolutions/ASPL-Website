About ASPL

Altener Solutions Private Limited (ASPL) is a Bangalore-based electric mobility company focused on developing reliable and scalable EV 3-wheeler platforms. Our solutions are engineered to support India’s fast-growing urban transportation and last-mile logistics ecosystem with sustainability and efficiency at the core.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To contribute meaningfully to India's electric mobility transformation by delivering dependable and future-ready EV solutions.

Our Mission

To design and scale intelligent electric 3-wheeler platforms that redefine commercial and passenger mobility.

Product Focus

Advanced lithium battery integration with enhanced lifecycle and safety.
